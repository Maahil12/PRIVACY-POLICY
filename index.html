1. Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy for Manta Bot. This policy outlines the types of data Manta Bot collects and how it is used. Manta Bot is committed to protecting the privacy of its users and ensuring a safe environment for everyone.

2. Data Collection

Manta Bot collects the following types of data:

  • User IDs: Unique identifiers for Discord users. This is collected to keep the bot well-maintained and provide personalized experiences.
  • Channel IDs: Identifiers for Discord channels. This is collected to keep the bot well-maintained and ensure proper functionality across different channels.
  • Guild IDs: Identifiers for Discord servers (guilds). This is collected to keep the bot well-maintained and provide server-specific features.

3. Data Usage

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing moderation features to maintain a safe and positive environment within Discord servers.
  • Enhancing the functionality of the bot for your server, such as providing entertainment features, managing server settings, and more.
  • Improving the overall user experience by analyzing usage patterns and feedback to make informed decisions for future updates and improvements.

4. Conclusion

Manta Bot is designed to provide fun and moderation features for your Discord server. Rest assured, we prioritize user privacy and only collect the necessary data for bot functionality. We are committed to maintaining transparency and providing a secure and enjoyable experience for all users.

For any concerns or questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at stormtownmaahil@gmail.com or join our support server.
